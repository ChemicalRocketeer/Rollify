Rollify

An app for managing stats and rolling dice in tabletop games

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Stop looking up tables, and start playing!

Rollify manages and tracks all of your character's stats and active effects for you, allowing you to use them in any way your game requires. Have a magic device that uses your intelligence combined with your stamina to damage the enemy for a percentage of his mana, while also draining your power points? Rollify will calculate everything for you at the touch of a button. Need to combine multiple checks? No problem. Rollify's flexible system adapts to your game and your play style.
