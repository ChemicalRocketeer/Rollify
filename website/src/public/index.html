Rollify

Stop looking up tables, and start playing!

Rollify manages and tracks all of your character's stats and effects for you, allowing you to use them in any way your game requires. Need to combine your intelligence with your stamina to damage the enemy for a percentage of his mana, while draining your power points? Rollify will calculate everything for you at the touch of a button. Rollify's flexible system adapts to your game and your play style.

Rollify is in development. Check back soon for more details!

Rollify is being developed by David Aaron Suddjian, at DigitalRocketry.
